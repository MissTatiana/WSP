Client Discovery

The Plaza Live is a business website for a local Orlando Bar that provides live entertainment from local and gloabal bands and musicians. The business allows space to be rented, and contacted if needed. Events are viewable the on the website and tickets are available for purchase through an online ticket vender.

Client Intent

Informative site providing detail for evening events alllowing users to browser through events, place an evening, purchase tickets, get in contact with someone, get directions, go through photost, rent the place for an event, join a mailing list.

Client Goals

Generate revenew through events where profits are distributed between the musicains and the bar.

Competition

Other local bars and clubs the provide and book live shows.

  • Hard Rock Cafe
  • Orlando Arena
  • Firestone

User Profile

A typical user is both male and female between the ages of 18-50 who would access their site from desktop and/or mobile browsers. These users would browse the side looking for upcoming event to plan a night out. Users would either purchase tickets through the distributer, or pay at the door.
